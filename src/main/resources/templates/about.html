About Our Project

Welcome to our competency checker platform! This project is a part of our initiatives to utilize advanced technology in assessing the potential and competency of individuals in the field of programming and development.

Our platform works by presenting users with a set of problems or tasks in different programming related categories. As the user provides their solutions or answers, our system forwards these responses to an advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) model.

The AI is built with state-of-the-art machine learning techniques and has been trained on a wide range of correct and incorrect programming solutions. This allows it to effectively rate the user's answers in terms of correctness, efficiency, and even coding style.

Upon completion of the tests, the AI generates a comprehensive summary of the user's performance, identifying their strengths and areas for improvement. This serves as a valuable tool for users to understand their competency level and progress in their learning journey.

This project, being part of our Curriculum Vitae (CV), demonstrates our ability to integrate and apply complex technologies in practical, user-centric applications. We hope it provides users with valuable insight into their programming skills and aids in their personal and professional development.
